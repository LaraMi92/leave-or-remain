Books

Leave or Remain (2022)

When Anna, a pro-European event manager, meets Camelia, her counterpart from the top Brexit think tank in Brussels, she thinks this is the rudest woman she’s ever met. But is Camelia really all she appears to be?
A heartwarming and funny love story of first impressions, second chances, and learning to fight for what’s truly important.

About

Hello, I'm Philippa Pegg

A lesbian romance author living and working in Brussels.
All my stories are (and will be) set in this rainy country called Belgium.

So if you’re ever tired of reading about romances happening in the Highlands or other beautiful landscapes and are looking for ladies falling for each other while public transportation is on strike, you knocked on the right door!
